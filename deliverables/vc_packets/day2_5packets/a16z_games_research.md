## Investment Thesis

GAMES FUND ONE is founded on the belief that **games will play a pivotal role in defining how we socialize, play, and work over the next century**. The fund focuses on:

### Core Themes:

1. **Game Studios** - Games-as-a-service with rich, interactive social networks
2. **Games x Consumer** - Consumer apps built around player communities
3. **Infrastructure** - Tools and services powering virtual worlds and the Metaverse
4. **AI x Games** - Next-generation gaming experiences powered by AI

### Key Beliefs:
- Games are evolving from packaged entertainment to online services resembling social networks
- Games are driving innovation across consumer tech (engagement, retention, monetization)
- Games infrastructure will be key building blocks of the Metaverse
- AI is redefining how we socialize, play, and work
