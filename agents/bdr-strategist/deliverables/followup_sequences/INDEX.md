## Follow-Up Strategy

### Day 3 Follow-Up
- **Timing:** 3 days after initial outreach
- **Length:** 75-100 words
- **Tone:** Professional, value-add
- **Content:** References original hook, adds new insight

### Day 7 Follow-Up
- **Timing:** 7 days after initial outreach
- **Length:** 100-125 words
- **Tone:** Social proof oriented
- **Content:** Metrics, case studies, traction updates

### Day 14 Follow-Up
- **Timing:** 14 days after initial outreach
- **Length:** 75-100 words
- **Tone:** Direct, respectful
- **Content:** Final ask, clear CTA, graceful close
