# Case Study: Daily Packet Engine → Partner Meetings

_Last updated: 24 Feb 2026_

## Problem
Gaming/AI founders were cold-emailing dozens of funds without context, leading to <5% reply rates and week-long approval cycles.

## Approach
- Built a scoring model that ranks 200+ funds daily on stage fit, check size, geo overlap, and thesis keywords.
- Automated packet generation so every morning Lucas gets five investor-ready briefs (snapshot, personalization, CTA) inside Trello + Slack.
- Logged approvals and replies back into the CRM + Trello for compounding data on what resonates.

## Outcomes (Needs Verification)
- Reply rate lifted from 7% → **30%** on the first 20 packets.
- **4 partner meetings/week** scheduled from the last two weeks of sends.
- Approval turnaround down to **<4 hours** because Lucas only reviews contextualized packets.
