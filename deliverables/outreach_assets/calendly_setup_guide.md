# Calendly Setup Guide: 30-Min Partner Intro Slot

**Purpose:** Enable prospects and partners to book intro calls without back-and-forth scheduling.

---

## Step 1: Create the Event Type

1. Go to [calendly.com](https://calendly.com) and sign in (or create account with lucas@yourdomain.com)
2. Click **"New Event Type"** → **"One-on-One"**
3. Configure basics:
   - **Event name:** "Partner Intro Call — 30 Min"
   - **Duration:** 30 minutes
   - **Location:** Video conferencing (Zoom/Meet auto-generated)

---

## Step 2: Configure Availability

Set your preferred windows (example for CST timezone):

| Day | Time Window |
|-----|-------------|
| Tuesday | 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM CST |
| Thursday | 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM CST |
| Friday | 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM CST |

**Buffer settings:**
- Minimum notice: 24 hours
- Buffer before/after: 15 minutes
- Maximum bookings per day: 3

---

## Step 3: Set Up Confirmation & Reminders

**Booking confirmation email:**
- Include calendar invite automatically
- Add brief agenda: "Quick intro, understand your goals, explore fit"

**Reminders:**
- 24 hours before (email)
- 1 hour before (email or SMS if enabled)

---

## Step 4: Customize Booking Page

**Public link:** `calendly.com/lucas/partner-intro-30min`

**Description:**
```
Quick 30-minute intro to explore partnership opportunities.

Agenda:
- Your current priorities and challenges
- How we might work together
- Next steps if there's mutual fit

You'll receive a calendar invite with video link upon booking.
```

**Questions to ask (optional but recommended):**
1. Company/Organization name
2. What prompted your interest?
3. Best phone number (backup contact)
