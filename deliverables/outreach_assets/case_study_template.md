## Usage Guidelines

### For Investor Decks
- Use "At-a-Glance" + "Results" sections only
- Include logo (with permission)
- Lead with the highest-impact metric

### For Sales Collateral
- Full version with challenge + solution
- Include customer quote prominently
- Add "Similar companies" context if relevant

### For Website
- Abridged version (Challenge → Solution → Results)
- Include customer headshot if available
- Link to full PDF case study

## Appendix: Customer Interview Questions

Use these to gather content for the case study:

**Background:**
1. Walk me through your situation before we started working together.
2. What were you trying to accomplish?
3. What was the cost of not solving this?

**Decision:**
4. How did you evaluate solutions?
5. What made you choose us over alternatives?
6. What were your concerns going in?

**Implementation:**
7. What did the rollout process look like?
8. How long until you saw initial results?
9. What was the team's reaction?

**Results:**
10. What specific metrics changed?
11. What surprised you about the outcome?
12. How has this impacted your broader goals?

**Future:**
13. What would you tell someone considering us?
14. What are you focusing on next?
