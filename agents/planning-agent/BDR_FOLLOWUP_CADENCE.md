# BDR Follow-up Cadence

## Overview

**Total BDR Cards Ready:** 71
**Expected Send Window:** 3-4 business days
**Follow-up Sequence:** 4-touch system over 21 days

---

## Follow-up Timeline

### Touch 1: Initial Outreach (Day 0)
**Status:** Card moves from "Ready for Review" → "Sent" upon approval + send

**Template Type:** Personalized cold outreach
**Send Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9:00 AM or 2:00 PM recipient time
**Expected Reply Window:** 48-72 hours

**Trello Actions:**
- Move card to "Sent - Awaiting Reply"
- Add due date: Day 3 (follow-up reminder)
- Add label: "Touch-1"

---

### Touch 2: First Follow-up (Day 3)
**Trigger:** No reply after 72 hours

**Template Type:** Brief bump with added value
**Subject:** "Re: [Original Subject]"

**Content Framework:**
```
Hi [Name],

Quick bump on my note below. I know priorities shift, so no worries if
now isn't the time.

[ADDED VALUE: one-line insight, relevant news, or shared connection]

Worth a brief chat?

[Your name]
```

**Send Time:** Same day of week as initial, 48 hours later
**Trello Actions:**
- Update due date: Day 7
- Change label: "Touch-2"
- Note: "First follow-up sent [DATE]"

**Decision Points:**
- Reply received → Move to "In Conversation"
- Out-of-office → Pause, resume when they're back
- Still no reply → Proceed to Touch 3

---

### Touch 3: Second Follow-up (Day 7)
**Trigger:** No reply after 7 days total

**Template Type:** Pattern interrupt or social proof
**Subject:** "Quick question about [Company Name]"

**Content Framework (Choose One):**

**Option A: Social Proof**
```
Hi [Name],

I wanted to share that [Similar Studio] just saw [specific result]
after implementing [relevant solution].

Given [their company]'s focus on [specific area], thought this might
be relevant to your roadmap.

Still open to a brief chat this week?
```

**Option B: Pattern Interrupt**
```
Hi [Name],

I'll be direct - I've reached out twice and haven't heard back.
Usually means one of three things:

1. You're not the right person for this conversation
2. The timing is off
3. It's not a priority right now

Totally fine either way - just let me know which camp you're in
and I'll adjust accordingly.

[Your name]
```

**Send Time:** Different day than previous touches
**Trello Actions:**
- Update due date: Day 14
- Change label: "Touch-3"
- Note: "Second follow-up sent [DATE], used [approach]"

---

### Touch 4: Final Follow-up / Breakup (Day 14)
**Trigger:** No reply after 14 days total

**Template Type:** Permission to close or alternative path
**Subject:** "Should I close the loop?"

**Content Framework:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times about [specific topic] and haven't
heard back. Rather than keep bothering you, I'll assume timing
isn't right.

I'll close your file for now, but if [topic] becomes a priority
in the future, feel free to reach out.

Best of luck with [specific project/launch/milestone you found
in research].

[Your name]
```

**Trello Actions:**
- Move card to "Nurture / Closed"
- Change label: "No Reply - Closed"
- Remove due date
- Note: "Breakup email sent [DATE], moved to nurture"

**Re-engagement Rule:**
If they reply to breakup email → Move to "In Conversation" immediately
If they don't reply → Keep in nurture list for 90-day check-in

## Response Handling

### Immediate Response Scenarios

| Response Type | Action | Trello Move | Next Step |
|---------------|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Interested / "Tell me more" | Reply within 4 hours | "In Conversation" | Book meeting |
| "Not now" | Ask for better timing | "Nurture / Later" | Set reminder for [date] |
| "Not interested" | Thank them, ask why | "Closed - Not Interested" | Log objection |
| "Wrong person" | Ask for referral | "Referral Requested" | Follow up in 3 days |
| Out-of-office | Note return date | "Paused - OOO" | Resume on return date |
| "Send info" | Send one-pager, request call | "Materials Sent" | Follow up in 3 days |

### Conversation Flow (Once Replied)

**Goal:** Book 15-minute intro call within 3 messages

**Message 1:** Their reply
**Message 2:** Your response with specific ask
```
Thanks for getting back to me, [Name].

[Answer their question briefly]

Would a brief 15-minute call next [Tuesday/Wednesday] work to
explore this further? I can work around your schedule.
```

**Message 3:** Calendar link or time confirmation
- If they don't propose time: Offer 2-3 specific slots
- If they ask for info first: Send brief deck, request call to discuss
