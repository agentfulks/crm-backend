## 3. Disqualification Filters
- Corporate / strategic investors that require product exclusivity or long diligence cycles.
- Funds with minimum check ≥$10M unless they operate dedicated seed programs.
- Dormant Web3/crypto funds with no 2023–2026 portfolio activity.
- Funds without human contact data (only generic forms) unless strong strategic fit.
- Regions requiring local incorporation or majority local ownership.

| **Funds with only web forms** (weak fit) | No human contact data + weak thesis alignment | Cannot execute timely outbound; low conversion probability |
| **Geographic-constrained funds** | Require local incorporation | Operational overhead not justified for Seed stage

### Leading → Lagging Traceability
1. **Coverage (Input)** drives ability to create 5 packets/day.
2. **Packet throughput (Process)** ensures Lucas sees consistent volume.
3. **Approvals + reply rate (Output)** produce meetings and eventual commitments.

## 5. Measurement & Instrumentation

- **Trello Automation Hooks**

  - Lucas approval (checkbox + move to "Approved / Send") → record `approved_at` timestamp.
  - Card to "Follow-up" → create CRM task due in 48h.

## 6. Data Quality Observations
- CSV contains placeholder emails (`user@domain.com`, `name@example.com`). Flag these for enrichment; treat as missing contact data.
- Stages use mixed taxonomy ("3. Early Revenue" etc.). Normalize into {Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A, Series B+}. Use regex mapping during ingestion.
- Countries column mixes regions and states (e.g., "California"). Map to ISO country codes and mark "Global" as multi-region.
- Some rows missing HQ; fill from LinkedIn scraping or Crunchbase.
