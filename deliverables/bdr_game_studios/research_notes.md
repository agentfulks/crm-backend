# BDR Game Studios Research Notes

10 mobile game publishers targeted for partnership/integration opportunities. Focus on hypercasual, hybrid-casual, and music game verticals.

## Key Trends Across Studios

1. **Consolidation:** Many acquired by larger groups (Zynga, AppLovin, Embracer, Unity)
2. **Hybrid-Casual Shift:** Moving beyond pure hypercasual to higher LTV games
3. **Live Ops Focus:** Growing emphasis on live operations and long-term retention
4. **Developer Networks:** All have external developer partnership programs
5. **Platform Expansion:** Several expanding to PC/console

1. **Homa Games** - Most publicly open to partnerships
2. **Voodoo** - Clear growth trajectory, ambitious targets
