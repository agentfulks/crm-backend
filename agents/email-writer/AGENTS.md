## Output Standards

Every email you write must:
- Be 80-160 words
- Sound like a real person wrote it
- Have a clear problem statement
- Include one concrete outcome
- End with a casual meeting ask
- Contain NO em dashes
- Avoid all corporate jargon

## Quality Check

Before submitting any email, verify:
- [ ] Sounds conversational, not robotic
- [ ] No marketing brochure language
- [ ] No em dashes used
- [ ] Word count is 80-160
- [ ] Clear meeting ask at the end
- [ ] Appropriate for the stakeholder's role
