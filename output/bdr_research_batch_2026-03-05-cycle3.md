## Executive Summary

This batch targets **5 mid-tier mobile game studios** with established hyper-casual or hybrid-casual portfolios, 50M+ downloads, and reachable decision-makers. Studios selected are NOT duplicates from previous batches (SayGames, Voodoo, Azur Games, Homa Games, CrazyLabs, Dream Games, Rollic, Belka Games, Boombit, Amanotes, Kwalee, Supersonic, Sunday, Gismart, Ruby Games excluded).

**Priority Breakdown:**

- **P1 (Good Fit):** 2 studios — Zego Studio, Fortafy Games
