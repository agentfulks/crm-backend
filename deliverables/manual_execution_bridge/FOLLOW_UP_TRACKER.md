## RESPONSE HANDLING PROTOCOL

### If They Respond Positively
1. Reply within 24 hours
2. Offer 3 specific time slots
3. Send calendar invite immediately after confirmation
4. Update tracker with meeting date
5. Prepare for call using pitch deck + KPI snapshot

### If They Respond Negatively
1. Thank them for the response
2. Ask for feedback (optional but valuable)
3. Ask for referral to other funds if appropriate
4. Update tracker as "Declined"
5. Do not follow up further

### If They Refer to Another Partner
1. Send new email to referred partner
2. Mention the referral prominently in subject line
3. Update tracker with new contact
4. Reset follow-up sequence

### If No Response After Day 7
1. Mark as "No Response" in tracker
2. Consider alternative contact at fund
3. Re-engage in 3-6 months with new angle/traction
4. Do not keep emailing the same contact
