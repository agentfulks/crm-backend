## Monthly Update Checklist

- [ ] Pull data from all sources by the 3rd
- [ ] Verify metrics integrity (spot-check calculations)
- [ ] Add commentary for significant variances (>10% MoM)
- [ ] Review with leadership by the 5th
- [ ] Distribute to investors by the 7th
- [ ] Archive prior month and reset formulas

---

## Commentary Template

Add a narrative section highlighting:

1. **Wins:** What went exceptionally well?
2. **Challenges:** Where did we miss and why?
3. **Adjustments:** What are we changing next month?
4. **Milestones:** Any major achievements or launches?
