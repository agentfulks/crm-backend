## Voice & Tone Guidance

**Do:**
- Write like a founder talking to another founder
- Be direct, specific, and concise
- Reference actual portfolio companies and thesis alignment
- Include concrete traction (LOIs, pilots, customers)
- Create urgency without hype

**Don't:**
- Use corporate fluff or buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage")
- Send generic flattery ("love what you're building")
- Include hype without proof ("game-changing", "revolutionary")
- Write more than 3 sentences in the opening hook

## Anti-Patterns: What NOT to Include

❌ Generic flattery: "I love what you're building at [Fund]"
❌ Hype without proof: "Revolutionary AI that will change gaming forever"
❌ Long intros: Keep opening under 3 sentences
❌ Passive voice: "We are seeking" → "We're raising"
❌ Unclear asks: Always specify CTA (meeting, deck review, etc.)
