## Strategic Analysis

### 1. Griffin Gaming Partners — Nick Tuosto (93/100)

**Thesis Alignment:**
Griffin is the largest VC fund dedicated exclusively to gaming ($1B+ AUM). Nick Tuosto's Goldman Sachs and LionTree background brings institutional capital discipline to gaming investments. Their portfolio includes Scopely ($5.8B exit) and Mythical Games, demonstrating success across traditional and Web3 gaming.

**Strategic Value:**
- Pure-play gaming focus — no distractions from other sectors
- Deep LP network includes major game publishers
- Series A to Growth expertise for scaling game companies
- Understanding of AI's role in game development and operations

**Outreach Angle:**
Position as AI infrastructure built specifically for game developers — not general-purpose AI tools retrofitted for gaming. Reference Scopely's AI-driven live ops success and Mythical's blockchain/AI intersection.

**Differentiation Hook:** Developer-first AI infrastructure that respects creative vision
