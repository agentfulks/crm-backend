## Investment Thesis

Makers Fund is a **global interactive entertainment venture capital firm** focused exclusively on gaming and interactive media. Their approach:

### Core Focus:

1. **Interactive Entertainment** - Games and game-adjacent experiences
2. **Early-Stage Founders** - Product inception through global scaling
3. **Long-Term Capital** - Patient capital for creative businesses
4. **Operational Support** - Hands-on support from industry veterans

### Key Beliefs:
- We're entering the "golden age for interactive entertainment"
- Early-stage founders need more than just capital
- Global gaming market requires global perspective (presence in US, EU, Asia)
- Team of former Riot Games, Activision Blizzard leaders
