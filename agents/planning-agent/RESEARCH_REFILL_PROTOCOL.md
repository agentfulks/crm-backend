## Daily Research Workflow (10 Studios/Day)

### Morning Block (60-90 minutes): Research & Enrichment

**Step 1: Source Selection (5 min)**
- Check LinkedIn alerts first
- Review overnight industry news
- Check funding announcement aggregators

**Step 2: Company Discovery (30 min)**
- Pull 15-20 companies from various sources
- Quick-filter against criteria (size, location, status)
- Narrow to 10 high-potential targets

**Step 3: Decision Maker Hunt (30 min)**
- LinkedIn search for each company
- Identify 1-2 key decision makers
- Verify titles and seniority
- Check mutual connections

**Step 4: Contact Enrichment (15 min)**
- Find work emails (Apollo, Hunter, RocketReach)
- Verify email format (test@company.com)
- Cross-reference Twitter/X for additional context

### Afternoon Block (60 minutes): Personalization & Card Creation

**Step 5: Deep Research (30 min)**
For each of 10 studios:
- Read latest news/announcements
- Check recent LinkedIn posts from decision maker
- Review company website and portfolio
- Identify personalization angles (3-5 per card)

**Step 6: Trello Card Creation (30 min)**
Create cards with:
```
Title: [Company Name] - [Decision Maker Name] - [Title]

Description:
Company: [Name]
Website: [URL]
Size: [XX employees]
Location: [City, Country]
Recent News: [Key insight]

Decision Maker:
Name: [Full name]
Title: [Title]
LinkedIn: [URL]
Email: [Email]
Background: [Relevant history]

Personalization Angles:
1. [Specific detail]
2. [Recent achievement]
3. [Shared interest/connection]

Target Tier: [1, 2, or 3]
Sourced From: [Channel]
Date Researched: [Date]
```

**Labels:**
- Type: BDR
- Priority: P1, P2, or P3
- Status: Ready for Review
- Workstream: BDR

## Quality Control Checklist

### Before Creating Card, Verify:

- [ ] Company matches target criteria (size, funding, location)
- [ ] Decision maker title is correct and current
- [ ] Email is verified or highly likely (pattern match)
- [ ] Recent company news exists (within 90 days)
- [ ] Personalization angle is specific and verifiable
- [ ] Not a duplicate (check existing cards)
- [ ] Not a competitor (don't target tools vendors)
- [ ] Not a current customer (check CRM)

### Quality Red Flags (Reject):

- Generic company with no recent news
- Decision maker not at company anymore
- No email found after 3 attempts
- Company clearly wrong fit (casual mobile, casino, etc.)
- Duplicate of existing card
- Obvious spam trap email
