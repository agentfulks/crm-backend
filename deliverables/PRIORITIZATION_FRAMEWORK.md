## 5. PRIORITIZATION DECISION MATRIX

### Quick-Score Algorithm

Use this for rapid triage of new targets:

```
IF (thesis = AI + gaming) AND (stage = seed/pre-seed) AND (check = $250K-$2M):
    → TIER 1 (Immediate)
    
IF (thesis = AI OR gaming) AND (stage = seed/Series A) AND (track_record = yes):
    → TIER 2 (Queue)
    
IF (thesis = adjacent) OR (stage = Series B+):
    → TIER 3 (Backlog)
    
IF (no thesis fit) OR (stage = growth/PE):
    → REJECT (Remove)
```

### Override Conditions

**Force to Tier 1:**
- Warm intro available
- Fund just announced new gaming/AI investment
- Partner engaged on social/LinkedIn

**Force to Tier 3 (or reject):**
- Fund hasn't invested in 18+ months
- Portfolio company is direct competitor
- Partner left, new partner unknown

## 8. QUALITY VS. VELOCITY TRADEOFFS

### The 80/20 Rule Applied

| Effort | Output | Outcome |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 20% effort | Tier 1 only (20 sends) | Likely 1-2 meetings |
| 40% effort | Tier 1+2 (50 sends) | Likely 2-4 meetings |
| 100% effort | All 150 sends | Likely 3-6 meetings |

**Insight:** Tier 1+2 (50 sends, 33% of total) likely generates 70% of meetings.

### Recommendation

Focus on **Tier 1+2 (50 funds)** first:
- Highest probability of reply
- Best strategic fit
- Manageable manual workload
- Data to optimize before scaling

Only proceed to Tier 3 after:
- Tier 1+2 fully processed
- Reply patterns analyzed
- Automation available

## 9. DYNAMIC REPRIORITIZATION

### Weekly Review Process

Every Friday, reassess:

1. **Reply Rate by Tier:**
   - Tier 1: Target 10-15% reply rate
   - Tier 2: Target 5-10% reply rate
   - Tier 3: Target 3-5% reply rate

2. **Adjust if needed:**
   - If Tier 1 reply rate <5% → Review messaging, not targeting
   - If Tier 2 reply rate >10% → Move top 10 Tier 3 funds to Tier 2
   - If Tier 3 reply rate <3% → Pause Tier 3, focus on 1+2

3. **Fund-Specific Triggers:**
   - Fund announces new AI/gaming investment → Bump to Tier 1
   - Partner posts about thesis → Engage on social first, then email
   - Portfolio company raises → Reference in outreach
