- **Tier-1 studios** (Dream Games, Wildlife, Moon Active) should be prioritized - they have independent decision-making, recent growth signals, and clear live ops needs

- All Tier-1 studios have recently raised funding or restructured, indicating budget availability
- Personalization hooks should reference specific revenue milestones, recent funding, or leadership transitions
