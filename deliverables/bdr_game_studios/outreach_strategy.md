# Game Studios Outreach Strategy — 2026-02-26

## Executive Summary

**Objective:** Introduce AI-assisted investor sourcing to 50 researched mobile/small game studios, positioning warm intros to gaming VCs as value-add.

**Target:** Studios 11-200 employees, mobile gaming focus, recent activity (2024-2025 releases/funding)

**Value Proposition:** We know which gaming VCs are actively investing and can surface your deck to the right partners faster.

---

## Outreach Angle

**Primary Hook:** "Know which gaming VCs are actively deploying capital"

Game studios spend months researching investors. We score 200+ gaming VCs daily and know:
- Who just closed new funds (dry powder)
- Who's actively doing gaming deals
- Check sizes and stage alignment
- Warm intro paths through our network

**Secondary Hook:** Portfolio value, not just capital

Beyond warm intros, we provide:
- Investor packet formatting (what VCs expect to see)
- Timing intelligence (when funds are actively deploying)
- Competitive landscape context (who else they're meeting)
