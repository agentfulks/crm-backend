## Investment Thesis

Griffin's mission is to be **"the definitive source of capital for the games industry."** They invest at the intersection of:

### Core Sectors:

1. **Content (Game Studios)** - Developers and publishers across all platforms
2. **Social Platforms** - Community and social gaming infrastructure
3. **Software Infrastructure** - Tools and tech powering game development
4. **Web3 Gaming** - Blockchain-enabled gaming experiences

### Key Beliefs:
- Gaming is the dominant entertainment medium of the 21st century
- Founder-friendly approach with deep operational support
- Long-term capital for building enduring gaming companies
- Cultural authenticity: "Of, By, and For The Culture"
