## Investment Thesis

Galaxy Interactive invests at the **intersection of content, technology and social commerce** with an emphasis on video games and the infrastructure powering immersive virtual worlds.

### Core Themes:

1. **Interactive Content** - Video games, immersive experiences
2. **Technology Infrastructure** - Tools powering virtual worlds
3. **Social Commerce** - Digital economies and marketplaces
4. **Spatial Computing** - VR/AR, Metaverse infrastructure
5. **Web3 & Blockchain Gaming** - NFTs, decentralized gaming economies

### Key Beliefs:
- Gaming is the entry point to the Metaverse
- Blockchain technology enables true digital ownership
- Content + technology convergence creates new platforms
- Virtual worlds require new infrastructure and tools
