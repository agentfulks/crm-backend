# BDR Weekly Cadence
## Game Studios Outreach Schedule — March 2026

**Volume Tolerance:** Studios are significantly more volume-tolerant than VCs. Game studios operate on rapid iteration cycles and are accustomed to frequent vendor outreach.  
**Recommended Pace:** Higher volume, faster follow-up than investor outreach.

---

## Weekly Send Volume

### Recommended: 20-25 Studios Per Week

| Metric | Value | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Weekly Volume** | 20-25 studios | Studios are less gate-kept than VCs; product/BD teams expect vendor outreach |
| **Daily Pace** | 4-5 studios/day (Mon-Thu) | Spread across 4 days to manage replies |
| **Follow-up Load** | ~40-50 emails/week including follow-ups | Day 3 and Day 7 bumps add 2x volume |
| **Pipeline Target** | 50 active conversations at any time | Rolling pipeline with 2-week rotation |

### Volume Tolerance by Studio Type

| Studio Type | Tolerance | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| **Hyper-casual Publishers** | Very High | Voodoo, Homa, SayGames receive constant vendor pitches; pattern-matching is normal |
| **Mid-core/Strategy** | Medium | Scopely, Nexters have formal BD processes; respect gatekeepers |
| **Indie/Small Studios** | High | Small teams (Innersloth, Wixel) are accessible but don't spam |
| **Public Companies** | Low-Medium | Boombit, MAG Interactive have formal processes; go through official channels |

## Follow-Up Schedule

### Standard 3-Touch Sequence (Faster Than VC Outreach)

```
Day 0 (Monday):    Initial outreach
Day 3 (Thursday):  First follow-up (bump + value add)
Day 7 (Monday):    Second follow-up (social proof or insight)
Day 14:            Close loop / Move to nurture
```

### Detailed Follow-Up Framework

#### Day 0: Initial Outreach
**Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday optimal (avoid Monday inbox flood, Friday wind-down)  
**Best Hours:** 9-11 AM recipient local time

| Component | Guideline |
|-----------|-----------|
| Subject | See A/B tests below |
| Length | 75-100 words |
| CTA | Single clear ask (15-min call, not "let me know") |
| Value | Lead with specific studio insight, not generic capability |

#### Day 3: First Follow-Up (Bump + Soft Value)
**Timing:** 3 business days after initial  
**Template Structure:**
```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

Quick bump — know you're busy.

[1-sentence value add: recent news, insight, or specific angle]

Worth 15 min to explore?

[Signature]
```

**Value Add Options:**
- Recent studio news: "Saw the Ducky Games acquisition — congrats"
- Industry insight: "Just saw Q1 mobile gaming trends — hyper-casual UA costs down 12%"
- Social proof: "Working with 3 other studios on similar challenges"

#### Day 7: Second Follow-Up (Social Proof or Insight)
**Timing:** 1 week after initial  
**Template Structure:**
```
Subject: [Studio name] + AI [specific use case]

[Name] — passing on one insight before I close the loop:

[Specific insight relevant to their business]

If this resonates, happy to share more. If not, no worries.

[Signature]
```

**Insight Examples by Angle:**
- **LiveOps:** "Studios using AI for live ops are seeing 15-20% retention lifts in Q1 2026"
- **UA:** "AI-generated creatives are outperforming human-made by 23% in our tests"
- **Monetization:** "Dynamic offer personalization = 8-12% ARPDAU lift for match-3 games"

#### Day 14: Close Loop or Nurture
**For non-responders:**
```
Subject: Closing the loop

[Name] — last note from me.

If AI-powered [specific use case] becomes a priority in the next quarter, feel free to reach out.

[Signature]
```

**For positive responses:** Move to Active Pipeline, schedule call within 48 hours.

## Subject Line A/B Test Suggestions

### Test Framework
Run A/B tests within each studio tier. Test 2 subject lines per batch of 10.

### By Outreach Angle

#### LiveOps Angle
| Variant | Subject Line | Hypothesis |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| A | `[Studio] + AI live ops automation` | Direct, specific |
| B | `Reducing live ops headcount without slowing releases` | Pain-focused |
| C | `15-min idea: AI content pipelines` | Low-commitment |

**Predicted Winner:** B for hyper-casual (headcount sensitivity), A for mid-core (specificity)

#### UA/Creative Angle
| Variant | Subject Line | Hypothesis |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| A | `AI creative generation for [studio genre]` | Capability-forward |
| B | `Cutting UA creative costs by 40%` | ROI-focused |
| C | `Faster creative testing at [studio] scale` | Scale-specific |

**Predicted Winner:** B for established studios, C for growth-stage

#### Monetization Angle
| Variant | Subject Line | Hypothesis |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| A | `Dynamic offer personalization for [game genre]` | Technical |
| B | `8-12% ARPDAU lift without SDK changes` | Specific outcome |
| C | `AI monetization: [studio] case study` | Social proof |

**Predicted Winner:** B (specific numbers outperform abstractions)

#### General/Partnership Angle
| Variant | Subject Line | Hypothesis |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| A | `Quick intro — AI tools for [studio]` | Soft intro |
| B | `[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out` | Warm intro (if applicable) |
| C | `15-min call: AI + [studio's recent news]` | News-jacking |

**Predicted Winner:** C (recency and relevance)

### Studio-Tier Specific Tests

#### Tier 1: Hyper-Casual Giants (Voodoo, Homa, SayGames)
Test: **Velocity vs. Scale**
- A: `Content velocity at [Studio] scale`
- B: `Scaling creative output without headcount`

#### Tier 2: AI-Adopters (TapNation, Supercent)
Test: **Expansion vs. Optimization**
- A: `Expanding your AI strategy beyond UA`
- B: `Optimizing [specific use case] with AI`

#### Tier 3: Strategic Acquirers (Scopely, Kwalee)
Test: **Portfolio vs. Platform**
- A: `AI across the [Studio] portfolio`
- B: `Platform-level AI integration`

## Weekly Rhythm

### Monday: Send Day
- Send Day 0 emails to new batch (5 studios)
- Review weekend replies from prior week
- Schedule calls from positive responses

### Tuesday: Send Day + Follow-Up Day
- Send Day 0 emails to new batch (5 studios)
- Send Day 3 follow-ups to previous Monday's batch

### Wednesday: Follow-Up Day
- Send Day 3 follow-ups to previous Tuesday's batch
- Reply to any responses

### Thursday: Send Day + Follow-Up Day
- Send Day 0 emails to new batch (5 studios)
- Send Day 7 follow-ups to previous week's batch

### Friday: Admin + Planning
- No new sends (low response rate)
- Update CRM/tracker
- Research next week's batch
- Prep email copy

## Response Handling Protocol

### Positive Response (Interested)
- Reply within 4 hours
- Propose 2 specific time slots (don't use "when works")
- Include Calendly as backup option
- Send calendar invite immediately upon confirmation

### Neutral Response (Curious but vague)
- Offer specific value: "Happy to share 3 examples of how [similar studio] is using this"
- Ask qualifying question: "What's your biggest content bottleneck right now?"
- Propose low-commitment next step: "15-min call to explore?"

### Negative Response (Not interested)
- Graceful close: "No problem — best of luck with [specific studio initiative]"
- Add to nurture list for 90-day follow-up
- Note reason in CRM for future reference

### No Response (After Day 14)
- Move to "Nurture" list
- Re-engage in 90 days with new angle or product update
- Exclude from active metrics
