## Key Insights

### Geographic Distribution
- **Turkey:** 4 studios (40%) - Istanbul is the hyper-casual capital
- **Europe:** 5 studios (50%) - Strong publishing ecosystem
- **Israel:** 1 studio (10%) - Tech-forward approach

### Stage Distribution
- **Early/Growth:** Grand Games, Good Job Games (recent pivots/funding)
- **Scale:** Dream Games, Kwalee, BoomBit (established players)
- **Corporate-owned:** Alictus (SciPlay), Green Panda (Ubisoft)

### Common Pain Points
1. Transition from hyper-casual to hybrid-casual (most studios)
2. Multi-platform expansion (mobile → PC/console)
3. Remote/distributed team coordination
4. Public company compliance (BoomBit, Alictus/SciPlay)
5. Franchise/LiveOps management at scale

### Recommended Outreach Sequence
1. Start with Tier 1 (Grand Games, Good Job Games, TapNation)
2. Use Tier 1 wins as social proof for Tier 2
3. Approach Tier 3 with highly customized angles
