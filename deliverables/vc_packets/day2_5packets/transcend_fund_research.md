## Investment Thesis

Transcend is a **top-performing early-stage venture capital firm** created by game industry veterans who have built digital entertainment companies for decades. Their focus:

### Core Focus:

1. **Games & Digital Entertainment** - Pure-play gaming fund
2. **Early-Stage** - Pre-seed and seed stage (first institutional check)
3. **Visionary Founders** - Partnering with ambitious founders building legendary games
4. **Platform Agnostic** - PC, mobile, console, VR/AR, blockchain

### Key Beliefs:
- "From Commodore to the cloud" - experience across all platform transitions
- Gaming is the most dynamic creative medium
- Early stage is about founder-market fit and vision
- Global perspective required (gaming is global)
