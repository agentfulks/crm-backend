# Tiered Approval Framework for Trello Outreach

**Purpose:** Reduce Lucas's approval time from ~12hrs/week to ~2hrs/week while maintaining quality control.

---

## Tier 1: Auto-Approve (60-70% of cards)
**Criteria:**
- Standard BDR outreach to game studios (non-competitors, no sensitive info)
- Follow-up sequences (Day 2, 7, 14) on previously approved initial outreach
- VC outreach using pre-approved templates (feedback ask, value-first)
- Cards with complete: target, hook, value prop, and CTA
- No custom research beyond standard firm/company profiles

**Action:** Cards meeting criteria auto-move to "Ready to Send"

---

## Tier 2: Batch Review (2x/week, 25-30% of cards)
**Criteria:**
- Strategic VC outreach (new firms, high-priority investors)
- Custom messaging requiring founder voice alignment
- New outreach angles or hooks being tested
- P0/P1 labeled cards with non-standard value props
- Partnership proposals (not pure BDR)

**Action:** Review in 15-min blocks, approve/reject/archive

---

## Tier 3: Individual Review (<10% of cards)
**Criteria:**
- Warm intro requests
- Competitor-adjacent outreach
- Funding conversations (explicit asks)
- Crisis/PR-sensitive situations
- Unusual asks or red flags

**Action:** Dedicated review with custom response
